Apple pie

ingredients

  • - butter - 200g
  • - flour - 100g
  • - 1 egg
  • - sugar - 50g
  • - apples - 1kg
  • - cinamonn - 1teaspoon

Apple pie recipe!

In a small bowl, combine the sugars, flour and spices; set aside. In a large bowl, toss apples with lemon juice. Add sugar mixture; toss to coat. Line a 9-in. pie plate with bottom crust; trim pastry even with edge. Fill with apple mixture; dot with butter. Roll out remaining pastry to fit top of pie. Place over filling. Trim, seal and flute edges. Cut slits in pastry.
